# Business & Product Subagents

Business & Product subagents help you make better product decisions, align teams, and deliver measurable outcomes. These specialists cover strategy, planning, customer growth, communication, and go-to-market execution.

## 🎯 When to Use Business & Product Subagents

Use these subagents when you need to:
- **Define product direction** and prioritize roadmap work
- **Gather requirements** and reduce ambiguity
- **Improve delivery flow** across teams and stakeholders
- **Strengthen customer outcomes** and retention
- **Create clear technical/product documentation**
- **Support sales conversations** with technical depth
- **Plan content and growth initiatives**
- **Address legal and compliance concerns**

## 📋 Available Subagents

### [**business-analyst**](./business-analyst/SKILL.md) - Requirements and process specialist
Turns stakeholder needs into actionable requirements and process improvements.

**Use when:** Clarifying requirements, mapping workflows, identifying gaps, or defining measurable business outcomes.

### [**content-marketer**](./content-marketer/SKILL.md) - Content strategy and growth specialist
Builds content strategies that improve visibility, engagement, and conversion.

**Use when:** Planning content calendars, improving SEO direction, refining messaging, or optimizing content performance.

### [**customer-success-manager**](./customer-success-manager/SKILL.md) - Retention and customer value specialist
Focuses on adoption, health signals, and expansion through stronger customer outcomes.

**Use when:** Designing success plans, improving retention, reducing churn, or scaling account health workflows.

### [**legal-advisor**](./legal-advisor/SKILL.md) - Legal and compliance specialist
Guides risk-aware decisions around contracts, privacy, IP, and regulatory exposure.

**Use when:** Reviewing legal constraints, shaping compliant workflows, or assessing policy and contract impacts.

### [**product-manager**](./product-manager/SKILL.md) - Product strategy and prioritization specialist
Drives product discovery, prioritization, and execution toward user and business value.

**Use when:** Defining roadmap priorities, writing product requirements, scoping features, or balancing trade-offs.

### [**project-manager**](./project-manager/SKILL.md) - Delivery planning and coordination specialist
Keeps execution on track with clear plans, ownership, and risk management.

**Use when:** Building delivery plans, coordinating cross-functional work, managing risks, or tracking timelines.

### [**sales-engineer**](./sales-engineer/SKILL.md) - Technical pre-sales specialist
Bridges technical detail and business value in demos, evaluations, and solution design.

**Use when:** Preparing technical demos, responding to buyer objections, validating fit, or supporting enterprise sales cycles.

### [**scrum-master**](./scrum-master/SKILL.md) - Agile delivery and team facilitation specialist
Improves team cadence, removes blockers, and strengthens agile execution.

**Use when:** Running Scrum ceremonies, improving sprint flow, coaching teams, or addressing process bottlenecks.

### [**technical-writer**](./technical-writer/SKILL.md) - Documentation clarity specialist
Produces clear, accurate docs for users, developers, and stakeholders.

**Use when:** Creating product docs, improving onboarding guides, documenting APIs/processes, or standardizing documentation quality.

### [**wordpress-master**](./wordpress-master/SKILL.md) - WordPress architecture and optimization specialist
Builds and scales WordPress solutions across themes, plugins, performance, and operations.

**Use when:** Designing WordPress architectures, building custom themes/plugins, improving performance, or scaling WordPress platforms.

## 💡 Common Combinations

**Product Discovery to Delivery:**
- **business-analyst** for requirements clarity
- **product-manager** for prioritization
- **project-manager** for execution planning
- **technical-writer** for artifacts and handoff quality

**Customer Growth Motion:**
- **customer-success-manager** for retention strategy
- **content-marketer** for lifecycle content
- **sales-engineer** for expansion/upsell technical support

**Governance and Risk-Aware Execution:**
- **legal-advisor** for compliance guardrails
- **project-manager** for controlled rollout
- **scrum-master** for team-level operational discipline

## 🎬 Getting Started

1. **Define the business outcome** you need to improve
2. **Pick the bottleneck** (strategy, execution, growth, or compliance)
3. **Choose the right specialist** from the list above
4. **Provide constraints and context** (timeline, stakeholders, risks)
5. **Iterate in short cycles** and measure impact

Choose your business & product specialist and move from ideas to outcomes faster.
